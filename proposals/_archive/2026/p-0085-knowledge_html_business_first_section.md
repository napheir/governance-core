drift, from `trade-agent`) via `/curate-candidate`. The drift carries a net-new
normative section **§2.5 「信息构建原则（业务优先）」** (~100 lines) authored on

(Art.11 package-source change; capability add → `/proposal`).

by context anchors**, NOT by the candidate's stale line numbers.
